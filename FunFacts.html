The first in Asia to legalize same-sex marriage

Following a Constitutional Court ruling and a subsequent legislative act, Taiwan became the first country in Asia to legalize same-sex marriage nationwide on 24 May 2019. Taiwanese embrace diverse culture and believe that everyone is born equal, but it still takes a very long fight to have the LGBT marraige legalize.

Elected first female president in 2016

Tsai Ing-wen made history in 2016 as Taiwan's first female president, and won second presidential term this year in early 2020. It was not an easy move for the country considering the traditional culture but a big win for equality and liberal democracy.

Taiwan is not Thailand lol

This might be one of the most common misunderstanding among non Asian people. Taiwanese famous national foods are Boba tea and Din Tai Fung XiaoLongBao, but not Pad Thai and Thai tea.
